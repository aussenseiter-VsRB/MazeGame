How to Play

Memorizing Phase (10s): Walls are visible. Memorize the path to the finish. You cannot move.

Move Phase (20s): Walls disappear. Use arrow keys to reach the finish without hitting walls.

You have 5 lives. Hitting a wall or running out of time costs 1 life.

Use the Hint button once per stage to reveal walls for 1 second.
